Freddie the Owl

Source: Instagram/freddietheowl

Freddie got named after the legendary rock singer Freddie Mercury, Queen.

  • Type: Barn-owl
  • Size: 24 cm
  • Country: Croatia
  • Family: Swoops (Ty, Beanie boos)

Freddie is a friendly owl who likes adventure. He is kind, cuddly, a bit clingy and a great friend. If things don't go his way, he can be a bit grumpy. Freddie likes to eat delicious food and is a big pizza lover. His favorite activity is playing hide and seek, in which he is very good at.
